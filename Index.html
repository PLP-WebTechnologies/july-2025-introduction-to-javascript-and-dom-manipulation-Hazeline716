Why Semantic HTML Matters

Semantic HTML elements provide meaning to web content, making it crucial for accessibility and search engine optimization (SEO).

Accessibility Benefits

For users relying on screen readers or other assistive technologies, semantic tags provide a roadmap of the page.

Key Tags:

  • <nav> for navigation links.
  • <main> for the primary content of the document.
  • <figure> and <figcaption> for images with captions.
